This app adds support for the Namron Z-wave.

Supported devices

Z-wave devices

- Wall controller 2 button
- Wall controller 4 button 2 channel
- Wall controller 4 button 1 channel
- Wall controller 8 button
- Wall controller 8 black button
- Remote 4 channel
- Dimmer (in-box module)
- Wall plug
- Dimmer (rotary)
- More Z-wave devices will follow soon

Supported Languages:

English
